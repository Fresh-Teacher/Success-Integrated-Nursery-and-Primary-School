ABOUT US

Success Integrated Nursery and Primary School is a mixed, private school. It is both a day and boarding school on international level but follows a national curriculum blended with International aspects. The pupils come from all over the world and from all religious backgrounds.

Location

Success Integrated Nursery and Primary School is found in Mpunga ward Kasangombe village qlong Kampala - Hoima road Mubiru stage in Wakiso Town Council- Kasangombe 100 meters off Mubiru Road.

Vision

To be a model school in Uganda that produces academically competitive, skilled and entrepreneurial learners.

Mission

Raising a Successful and God Fearing Generation.

Cultural Practise

Our school respects all people from different cultures and tribes. We also have pupils from different regions of the country with different nationalities and tribes like:

  • Baganda
  • Itesot
  • Banyankore
  • Banyarwanda
  • Langi

Practical Lessons

Our school delivers practicals lessons which are learner centred and involve active participation of pupils.

The learner centred approach helps pupils to grasp and master the content delivered to them.

Educational Tours

Our school organises educational tours and trips to different places of the country like:

  • Rwenzori Moutains National Park
  • Queen Elizabeth National Park
  • Kitagata Hotsprings

Such educational tours help pupils relate what they study in class with the real world.

Computer Lessons

We also conduct computer practical lessons at our school in order to equip our learners with technology skills which they need to apply in their day-to-day life because the world is changing so fast with new merging technologies every day!

We teach, among others, the following programs:

  • Microsoft Word
  • Microsoft Excel
  • Microsoft Powerpoint
